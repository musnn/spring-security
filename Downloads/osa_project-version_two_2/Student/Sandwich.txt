Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
2 slices of bread
2 slices of cheese
Butter

Instructions:
1.Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
2.Butter one side of each slice of bread.
3.Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on the pan.
4.Add cheese slices on top of the bread slice.
5.Place the other slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese.
6.Cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
7.Flip the sandwich over and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
8.Serve hot.
